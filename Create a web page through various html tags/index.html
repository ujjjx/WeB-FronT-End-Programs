University School Of Information And Communication Technology

About USICT

USICT, or University School of Information, Communication and Technology, is a part of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), a public university in Delhi. USICT was established in 1999 with the vision of becoming an internationally recognized centre for education and research in the field of information and communication technology. USICT offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in various branches of engineering and technology. USICT is located in the main campus of GGSIPU in Sector-16, Dwarka, which is a green and spacious area, well-connected by metro and road. USICT has highly qualified and experienced faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and industry-oriented curriculum.

The University School of Information, Communication & Technology has been established to design and implement courses with a twin objective of generating effective professionals and to keep pace with the R & D activities of this fast emerging and changing field of Information & Communication Technology.
The school aims at inculcating essential skills as demanded by the global software industry, through the interactive learning process. This includes team-building skills, audio-visual presentations and personality development programmes. These enhance analytic and communication skills, besides inculcating the virtues of self-study.

THRUST AREAS

The School was set up with an aspiration to generate manpower, with the ability to take on challenges and fare forward in the realm of Information & Communication Technology. The Curriculum has been designed to cater to the ever changing demands of information, communication technology, with the necessary inputs from the Industry. The School has highly qualified & experienced faculty who excel in their respective areas. The School also encourages research work in various areas of information & communication technology. The school aspires to produce individuals equipped to contribute for progress at all levels: individual, national and global.

Course Details

B.Tech. (Information Technology)

This course covers the fundamentals and applications of information technology, such as data structures, algorithms, operating systems, database management systems, software engineering, web development, networking, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. The duration of the course is four years and the admission is based on the rank in JEE (Main) through GGSIPU Counselling.

B.Tech. (Computer Science & Engineering)

This course covers the core concepts and principles of computer science, such as programming languages, compilers, automata theory, discrete mathematics, computer architecture, data mining, cloud computing, and distributed systems. The duration of the course is four years and the admission is based on the rank in JEE (Main) through GGSIPU Counselling.

B.Tech. (Electronics & Communication Engineering)

This course covers the design and analysis of electronic circuits, devices, and systems, such as analog and digital electronics, signals and systems, microprocessors, communication systems, control systems, and embedded systems. The duration of the course is four years and the admission is based on the rank in JEE (Main) through GGSIPU Counselling.

M.Tech. (Information Technology)

This course covers the advanced topics and research areas of information technology, such as data analytics, big data, internet of things, blockchain, cyber security, and natural language processing. The duration of the course is two years and the admission is based on the rank in GATE or CET conducted by GGSIPU

M.Tech. (Computer Science & Engineering

This course covers the state-of-the-art techniques and research problems of computer science, such as parallel and distributed computing, software engineering, computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. The duration of the course is two years and the admission is based on the rank in GATE or CET conducted by GGSIPU

M.Tech. (Electronics & Communication Engineering)

This course covers the modern technologies and research challenges of electronics and communication engineering, such as wireless communication, optical communication, digital signal processing, image processing, and nanoelectronics. The duration of the course is two years and the admission is based on the rank in GATE or CET conducted by GGSIPU.

M.Tech. (VLSI Design)

This course covers the design and fabrication of very large scale integrated circuits, such as CMOS technology, digital VLSI design, analog VLSI design, testing and verification, and low power VLSI design. The duration of the course is two years and the admission is based on the rank in GATE or CET conducted by GGSIPU.

M.Tech. (RF & Microwave Engineering)

This course covers the theory and practice of radio frequency and microwave engineering, such as electromagnetic theory, microwave devices, antennas, microwave circuits, and microwave systems. The duration of the course is two years and the admission is based on the rank in GATE or CET conducted by GGSIPU.

M.Tech. (Information Security)

This course covers the principles and techniques of information security, such as cryptography, network security, web security, digital forensics, and cyber laws. The duration of the course is two years and the admission is based on the rank in GATE or CET conducted by GGSIPU.

Ph.D. (Information Technology)

This course covers the original and significant research contributions in the field of information technology, under the guidance of a faculty supervisor. The duration of the course is variable and the admission is based on the performance in the entrance test and interview conducted by GGSIPU.

Ph.D. (Computer Science & Engineering)

This course covers the original and significant research contributions in the field of computer science and engineering, under the guidance of a faculty supervisor. The duration of the course is variable and the admission is based on the performance in the entrance test and interview conducted by GGSIPU.

Ph.D. (Electronics & Communication Engineering)

This course covers the original and significant research contributions in the field of electronics and communication engineering, under the guidance of a faculty supervisor. The duration of the course is variable and the admission is based on the performance in the entrance test and interview conducted by GGSIPU.
