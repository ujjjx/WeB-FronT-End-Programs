Statue Of Athena In Athens

The statue of Athena in Athens, known as Athena Parthenos, was a colossal chryselephantine (gold and ivory) sculpture standing over 12 meters (40 feet) tall. It was housed in the Parthenon on the Acropolis and depicted the goddess Athena, the patron deity of Athens, holding a shield in one hand and a statue of Nike (Victory) in the other.

Landscape in Greece

Greece's landscape boasts diverse terrains, from rugged mountains to pristine beaches, dotted with ancient ruins and picturesque villages. Olive groves, vineyards, and azure seas contribute to its breathtaking Mediterranean charm.

Landscape in Egypt

Egypt's landscape is dominated by the iconic Nile River, surrounded by vast stretches of desert, including the Sahara to the west and the Arabian Desert to the east. Along the Nile's fertile banks, ancient temples and monuments stand as testaments to the country's rich history and civilization.
